Modificare gli input con Join

In questa attività impareai a comporre nuove parole.
  1. If you haven't used the random animal block, try it in the animal input slot of the Super Short Story block now. What is a potential problem with using this block in this story?
  2. Find the block in the palette and drag it into the Scripts area. Click it, change the inputs, and click it again. What does the join block do?
  3. Drag the random animal block into the first slot of the join block and type an "s" in the second slot. Click it to test that it reports a plural animal, and then drag the entire join expression into the Super Short Story block.
In this activity, you learned how to join strings of text to refine the story outputted by the Super Short Story block.
